About Me

Who am I?

Hello there! I'm Niraj Dhakal, a curious and passionate individual navigating through life's adventures with enthusiasm and purpose. Born and raised in Palpa, Nepal, I've always had a thirst for knowledge and a love for exploration.

My journey is a tapestry woven with diverse experiences and interests. From graduating with honors in Electronics and Communication Engineering to embarking on a transformative solo trek through the breathtaking landscapes of the Himalayas, I've learned the value of resilience, adaptability, and empathy.

My Hobbies

Beyond the confines of work or study, you'll often find me immersed in a variety of hobbies and pursuits, including:

  • Exploring the rich cultural heritage of Nepal through travel and culinary adventures.
  • Expressing creativity through photography, capturing the awe-inspiring beauty of the Himalayan landscape.
  • Finding solace in nature through hiking, meditation, or simply enjoying a quiet moment by the riverside.
  • Engaging in community service and volunteering to uplift marginalized communities and contribute to sustainable development.
